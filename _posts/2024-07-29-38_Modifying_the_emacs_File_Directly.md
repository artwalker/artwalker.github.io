Simply invoke Emacs with the command-line option **-q**, and Emacs will not run your *.emacs* file.

[.emacs exsiting file](https://www.dotemacs.de/)

### Saving .emacs

1. Restart the emacs
2. **M-x load-file** **\~/.emacs Enter**
